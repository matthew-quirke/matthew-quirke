# Matt Quirke

Electrical / Electronics / RF / Embedded Systems

I work on real-world systems — diagnosing faults, analysing behaviour, and building reliable solutions across marine power, RF, and embedded platforms. Most of my work sits at the intersection of electrical, electronics, and software rather than pure development.

---

## 🔧 What I do

- Diagnose and repair complex electrical and electronic systems down to component level  
- Investigate transient behaviour in DC power systems (LiFePO₄, inductive loads, switching systems)  
- RF and radio troubleshooting (HF, VHF, AIS, SDR-based analysis)  
- Embedded and Linux-based systems (STM32, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, instrumentation)  
- Design and build practical monitoring and control systems  

- Design, installation, and troubleshooting of off-grid and marine power systems:  
  batteries (LiFePO₄, AGM), solar charging (MPPT), hydrogenerators, and diesel generator integration  
- Configuration and fault diagnosis of Victron-based energy systems (charging, inverters, system integration)
